Who we are

Built by students.
For every student.

AGSB Speaks is an independent, student-run platform. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the school or its staff in any way.

Why we exist

We are an anonymous group of students who believe the school does not properly represent our views. Decisions that directly affect our daily lives — timetables, rules, policies — are made without meaningful consultation. We are told, not asked.

Pupil Parliament exists, but it doesn't work. We have spoken to too many form captains across the school and the message is consistent: student concerns raised through official channels are not acted on. The structure exists to give the appearance of consultation, not to deliver it. Its fundimentally flawed and has failed to serve students- we beleive a more hand-on, data-driven and grassroots approach is needed to make the case for change.

AGSB Speaks exists to change that. We built this platform because students deserve a place to make their views known — independently, transparently, and without fear of consequences.

Why we are anonymous

We are anonymous to protect ourselves. Students who have raised concerns through official channels have faced pushback. We want to be able to operate freely and honestly without the risk of being targeted or silenced.

This anonymity does not reduce our accountability. Every poll result, every petition count, and every piece of data we publish is real and verifiable. We will never fabricate numbers. When we present findings to the school, the data will speak for itself.

How it works

Polls : we run regular polls on school policies and decisions. Anyone with an account can vote. Results are visible to everyone, in real time, with no login required to view them.

Petitions : when a policy needs to change, we open a petition. Signatures are counted as a percentage of the total student membership, giving the school a clear picture of how widely a view is held.

Articles : students can submit opinion pieces and analysis anonymously. Every submission is reviewed before publishing. We maintain editorial standards: no personal attacks, no unverified claims — just evidence and argument.

What we will do with the data

When our polls and petitions reach a clear majority, we will present our findings to relevant sources of authority in the school. We will publish everything we submit, so students can see exactly what we said and what response we received.

We are not here to cause disruption. We are here to make the case: clearly, professionally, respectfully, and backed by data.Our student voice matters and must be taken seriously.

We hope to foster a culture of open dialogue and evidence-based decision making at AGSB. Change won't happen overnight, but with consistent effort and clear data, we believe we can make a real difference.

We have no affiliation with any other groups or organisations in or out of school.
